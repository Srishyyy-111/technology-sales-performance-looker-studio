# Technology Sales Performance - Looker Studio

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing the sales performance of a technology-based business using **Google Looker Studio**. The objective of this project was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive and business-focused dashboard that helps stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard provides insights into key business areas such as revenue performance, profitability, product contribution, regional performance, customer segments, and overall sales trends.

---

## Business Objective

The purpose of this analysis is to understand business performance and answer important stakeholder questions:

- How is the overall business performing?
- How is revenue changing over time?
- Which products and categories contribute the most revenue?
- Which regions generate the highest business value?
- Which customer segments contribute significantly to sales performance?

The dashboard was designed to provide a clear and interactive reporting experience for business users.

---

## Industry Context

**Industry:** Technology Solutions / IT Services

The dataset represents a technology business environment where sales transactions, customer information, product details, and financial metrics are analyzed to evaluate business performance.

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional business data including customer details, product information, sales transactions, and financial performance metrics.

Key fields include:

- Order ID
- Customer ID
- Order Date
- Product
- Category
- Region
- Customer Segment
- Quantity
- Revenue
- Cost
- Profit

The dataset was used to build interactive visualizations and generate meaningful business insights.

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

### Executive Overview

Provides a summary of business performance through key performance indicators (KPIs):

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold


---

### Sales Trend Analysis

Analyzes revenue performance over time to identify business growth patterns and sales movement.
---

### Regional Performance Analysis

Evaluates sales contribution across different regions to identify high-performing markets.

---

### Product Performance Analysis

Analyzes product-level contribution to identify products generating the highest revenue.

---

### Customer Segment Analysis

Examines customer groups and their contribution towards overall business performance.
